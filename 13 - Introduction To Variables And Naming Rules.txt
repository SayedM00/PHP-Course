Rules to declare a variable : 

1 - Starts with the dollar sign $.
2 - Starts with a letter from a-Z or underscore.
3 - You can use numbers but only in the middle and the end.
4 - You can't use special characters.
5 - Case sensitive.


** 
hint: when you use single quotes your variable won't work.
hint: when you use double quotes your variable will work.
